// ABOUT US

Together, we are greater than the sum of our parts. Corde represents a new level of excellence in the integrated design and delivery of building projects.

Corde is based on our belief that high quality, efficient design is a group effort. Together, our design response is more inspired, better considered, and more consistently delivered.

The reason is simple. Uniting architecture, structural engineering, and mechanical and electrical design, we avoid traditional 'silos' of expertise. Instead, we bring our staff together around a table. Working so closely together, questions can be answered more quickly, and ideas shared more easily. Right from the beginning, through every design stage, our teams are genuinely talking: asking questions, solving problems and sharing expertise.

We utilise Building Information Modelling (BIM) to create a responsive design solution. Models are adapted and adjusted in real-time, allowing our teams to progress rapidly. Integrated building data allows us to test and analyse performance, reducing risk and improving quality and deliverability.

For our clients, the result is a more agile and efficient process that doesn't compromise on creativity or quality.

// OUR PEDIGREE

Corde brings together three leading practices in the fields of architecture, structural, civil and environmental engineering, and mechanical and electrical building services.

With a shared vision from the start, architects and engineers from ADP, AKS Ward and KJ Tait work together in one location. We bring collective expertise to large and complex projects in both the public and private sectors, delivering award-winning buildings in education, research and life sciences, public and community, and commercial development.

With studios located throughout the UK, we are able to deliver a local service to clients nationwide.
